ABOUT US

Dbotica mobile application helps users to get medical prescriptions delivered to their doorstep. Users can track their medical history and can consult/refer to new doctors and Laboratories. Being a new technological advancement in the medical field, Dbotica provides economical and digital healthcare solutions. With an ever increasing scope the company is currently benefiting residents of Bangalore.
